Quick Answer: For emergencies, call 911. For non-emergency parish issues, call WBR Emergency Management at (225) 346-1577 or the WBR Sheriff at (225) 343-9234. For utilities, call WBR Parish at (225) 336-2406 or Port Allen at (225) 383-1620. Save this page for quick reference.
