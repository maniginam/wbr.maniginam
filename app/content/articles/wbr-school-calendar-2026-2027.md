Key breaks include Fall Break (Oct 12-13), Thanksgiving (Nov 23-27), Winter Break (Dec 21-Jan 1), Mardi Gras (Feb 8-10), and Spring Break (Mar 26-Apr 2). ## First Semester (Aug 6 - Dec 17)

      Teachers return for in-service on **August 3**, with students starting on **August 6, 2026**. The 1st Nine Weeks ends **October 9**, which is a teacher in-service day (students do not attend). **Fall Break is October 12-13**, giving families a long weekend.

      **Election Day on November 3** is a day off for students and staff. **Thanksgiving Break runs November 23-27**, covering the full week.

      The 2nd Nine Weeks and first semester wrap up on **December 17**. **December 18** is a teacher in-service day, and **Winter Break runs December 21 through January 1**. Students return on **January 6, 2027**.

      
## Second Semester (Jan 6 - May 21)

      **Martin Luther King Jr. Day** is observed on **January 18**. **Mardi Gras Break** falls on **February 8-10**, giving students Monday through Wednesday off for carnival.

      The 3rd Nine Weeks ends **March 12**, which doubles as a teacher in-service and asynchronous instructional day.

      **Spring Break runs March 26 through April 2**, wrapping around Good Friday (March 26) and Easter weekend. This is the longest break of the second semester.

      **Graduating seniors finish on May 7**. The school year ends for all other students on **May 21, 2027**. Teachers and support staff wrap up by **May 24**.
