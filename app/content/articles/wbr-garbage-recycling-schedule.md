**Quick Answer:** If you live outside Port Allen city limits, your garbage is collected by Waste Pro at (225) 744-6400. Inside Port Allen, call the City at (225) 383-1620. Recycling is every Wednesday for unincorporated areas and every Friday inside Port Allen. Bulk pickup rotates by area each week of the month.
      

      
## Outside Port Allen (Waste Pro)

      Residents in unincorporated West Baton Rouge Parish -- including Addis, Brusly, and surrounding areas -- are served by **Waste Pro**. Contact them at **(225) 744-6400** for any service questions.

      
### South of the Intracoastal Waterway

      
        
- **Garbage:** Monday and Thursday
        
- **Recycling:** Wednesday
      

      
### Sugar Mill Plantation Residents

      
        
- **Garbage:** Monday and Thursday
        
- **Recycling:** Wednesday
      

      
### North of the Intracoastal Waterway

      
        
- **Garbage:** Tuesday and Friday
        
- **Recycling:** Wednesday
      

      
## Inside Port Allen (City Collection)

      If you live within the City of Port Allen, your collection is handled by the city. Contact: **(225) 383-1620**.

      
        
- **Recycling:** Every Friday
        
- **Bulk Items:** Saturdays
      

      Place your carts curbside by 6:00 AM on collection day. Carts should be removed from the curb by the end of collection day.

      
## Bulk Pickup Schedule (Outside Port Allen)

      Bulk pickup for unincorporated WBR Parish is handled on a rotating weekly schedule by area:

      
        
          Week of MonthArea
        
        
          **1st Full Week**Addis
          **2nd Full Week**Brusly
          **3rd Full Week**Intracoastal north to Hwy 190
          **4th Full Week**North of Hwy 190 to Mississippi River
        
      

      Bulk items include furniture, appliances (with doors removed), mattresses, and large yard debris. Do not place bulk items curbside more than 24 hours before your scheduled pickup week.

      
## Reporting Issues

      Missed pickups, damaged carts, or other collection problems can be reported through the parish's **SeeClickFix** portal. You can submit requests online or through the SeeClickFix app. The parish monitors these reports and coordinates with Waste Pro for resolution.

      For urgent waste issues -- an overturned cart blocking the road, illegal dumping, or a hazardous spill -- call the WBR Parish at (225) 336-2406 directly rather than submitting a SeeClickFix request.

      
## Holiday Collection Changes

      Garbage and recycling collection schedules shift during major holidays. Waste Pro and the City of Port Allen typically observe the following holidays, which may delay your pickup by one day:

      
        
- **New Year's Day** (January 1)
        
- **Mardi Gras Day** (date varies)
        
- **Memorial Day** (last Monday in May)
        
- **Independence Day** (July 4)
        
- **Labor Day** (first Monday in September)
        
- **Thanksgiving Day** (fourth Thursday in November)
        
- **Christmas Day** (December 25)
      

      When a holiday falls on your regular collection day, service is typically pushed back one day for the remainder of that week. For example, if your normal pickup is Tuesday and the holiday is Monday, expect Tuesday collection on Wednesday instead. Confirm any changes by calling Waste Pro at (225) 744-6400 or checking the parish website.

      
## What You Can and Cannot Recycle in WBR

      Curbside recycling in West Baton Rouge Parish accepts single-stream recyclables in your green recycling cart. Accepted items include:

      
        
- Paper and cardboard (flattened)
        
- Plastic bottles and containers (#1 and #2 plastics)
        
- Aluminum and steel cans
        
- Glass bottles and jars
      

      Do **not** place the following in your recycling cart: plastic bags, styrofoam, food-contaminated containers, electronics, batteries, or yard waste. Contamination in the recycling stream can cause entire loads to be diverted to the landfill, so keeping your recycling clean matters.
