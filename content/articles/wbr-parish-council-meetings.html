Quick Answer: The WBR Parish Council meets the 2nd and 4th Thursday at 5:30 PM at 880 N. Alexander Ave. All meetings are open to the public. Agendas and minutes are posted at wbrparish.org/129/Agendas-Minutes, and many meetings are recorded on YouTube.

WBR Parish Council

DetailInformation

The Parish Council handles zoning changes, ordinances, budgets, road and drainage issues, and other parish-wide matters. Public comment periods are available at each meeting.

Planning & Zoning Commission

DetailInformation
Schedule1st and 3rd Tuesday of each month
Time5:30 PM

The Planning and Zoning Commission reviews subdivision plats, rezoning requests, variance applications, and other land-use matters before they go to the Parish Council for final approval.

Agendas, Minutes & Recordings

Parish Council agendas and minutes are posted online at wbrparish.org/129/Agendas-Minutes. Agendas are typically published several days before each meeting.

Many WBR Parish Council meetings are also recorded and available on YouTube. Check the parish's official YouTube channel for recordings of past meetings.

For municipal meetings (Port Allen, Addis, Brusly), contact the respective town hall for agenda availability. Port Allen agendas are sometimes posted on the city website at portallen.org.
